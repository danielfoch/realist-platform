# Topic: viral_sharing

## Overview
Covers the viral underwriting share system that rewards users with premium credits when recipients engage meaningfully with shared property analyses. Includes share creation, recipient tracking, action qualification, credit management, and growth analytics.

## Key Concepts
- Qualified share actions (unique_open, challenge, fork, signup, saved_version)
- Premium credit ledger and redemption
- Daily share and recipient caps
- Share lineage tracking for viral chains
- Growth nudges and conversion bottleneck detection
- Recipient coaching and funnel optimization
