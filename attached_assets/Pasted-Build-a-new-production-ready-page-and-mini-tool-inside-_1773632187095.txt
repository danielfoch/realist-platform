Build a new production-ready page and mini-tool inside the existing Realist.ca app called “Multiplex Investor Fit Assessment.”

Goal:
Create a page that feels like a helpful multiplex investor assessment tool, but is strategically designed as a conversion funnel that routes visitors to either:
1. buy Multiplex Masterclass Canada, or
2. book a free consult call for higher-ticket mentorship / advisory.

This should NOT feel scammy or overly salesy. It should feel diagnostic, useful, clean, and aligned with the Realist brand.

PAGE URL
- /multiplex-investor-fit

PRIMARY UX GOAL
- User takes a 3–5 minute assessment
- User sees a personalized result with a fit score and recommended path
- User is routed to the correct CTA:
  - Buy course
  - Book consult
  - Join waitlist / nurture

DESIGN DIRECTION
- Match existing Realist.ca visual language
- Clean, modern, investor-focused, slightly data-driven
- Should resemble a “tool” more than a generic sales page
- Include strong trust / credibility sections
- Mobile responsive
- Fast-loading

PAGE STRUCTURE
1. Hero
   - Headline: “Should You Be a Multiplex Investor?”
   - Subheadline: “Get a fast assessment based on your budget, market, timeline, and risk tolerance — then decide whether to self-study with Multiplex Masterclass Canada or book a free consult call.”
   - CTA button: Start the Assessment
   - Secondary CTA: See What’s Included

2. Trust strip
   - National framework for Canadian investors
   - Based on our existing Multiplex Masterclass curriculum
   - Built by the team behind Realist and The Canadian Real Estate Investor
   - Designed to help avoid zoning, financing, permitting, and execution mistakes

3. Short problem/benefit section
   - Explain that most people like the idea of multiplexes long before they know whether the strategy actually fits them.
   - The tool should position itself as a clarity engine, not a hype engine.

4. Multi-step assessment tool
   - 5 steps max
   - Progress bar
   - Minimal friction
   - Clean cards, toggles, dropdowns, and button selections
   - Final opt-in gate before result is fully revealed

QUIZ QUESTIONS
Step 1: Market
- Province (dropdown)
- City / target market (text)
- Investing in home market or remotely? (buttons)

Step 2: Goal
- What are you trying to accomplish? (multi-option single select)
  - House-hack
  - Add rental income
  - Long-term appreciation
  - Small development / build and hold
  - Just learning for now

Step 3: Capital and experience
- Approx liquid capital available? (button ranges)
  - Under 50k
  - 50k–100k
  - 100k–200k
  - 200k–400k
  - 400k+
- Current investing experience? (single select)
  - None
  - Own a rental
  - Have done renovations / BRRRR
  - Have done development / multifamily work

Step 4: Complexity tolerance
- Are you comfortable dealing with contractors / consultants / approvals? (scale or buttons)
- Are you open to delays and some execution risk? (yes/somewhat/no)
- Are you willing to invest outside your exact neighborhood if the economics are better? (yes/no/somewhat)

Step 5: Help preference
- Which sounds most like you?
  - I want a course and templates to do this myself
