PAGE 8 — DEAL MAP / QUICK FORMULAS
Purpose: practical cheat sheet for underwriting.

Content:

* Title: DEAL MAP
* Subtitle: “The numbers tell the story.”

Include clean formula cards:

Total Project Cost:
Purchase Price + Closing Costs + Hard Costs + Soft Costs + Financing Costs = Total Project Cost

NOI:
Gross Rent - Vacancy - Operating Expenses = Net Operating Income

Cap Rate:
NOI ÷ Purchase Price = Cap Rate

Yield on Cost:
Stabilized NOI ÷ Total Project Cost = Yield on Cost

Explain difference:
“Cap rate usually measures income yield against purchase price or current value. Yield on cost measures the income yield against what you actually spent to create the finished asset. For development and heavy value-add projects, yield on cost is often more useful.”

Cash-on-Cash Return:
Annual Pre-Tax Cash Flow ÷ Total Cash Invested = Cash-on-Cash Return

DSCR:
NOI ÷ Annual Debt Service = DSCR

Simple profit:
Projected Value - Total Project Cost = Project Profit

Buildable square footage rough check:
Lot Area × Permitted FSI = Estimated Gross Floor Area

Add caution:
“Always confirm zoning, GFA definitions, exclusions, parking, setbacks, and local rules with qualified professionals. Rough math is for screening, not final design.”

QR code:
https://realist.ca/tools/calculators
