Main thesis to support

The Ontario / GTA pre-construction market is weak enough that many builders are cutting prices, and the new GST/HST rebate gives them more room to do that. This is not bullish for the resale market. Better pricing and tax-adjusted affordability on new inventory likely pulls demand away from resale and puts downward pressure on resale pricing over the next year.

At the same time, do not overstate the claim that builders are broadly “capturing” the rebate. The report should show what the uploaded data actually supports:

cuts outnumber raises
cuts are deeper than raises
many prices are unchanged
unchanged prices can still imply improved affordability for buyers if rebate treatment improved
only a small number of increases appear to exceed the rough rebate benchmark

Use cautious language where causality is not fully proven.
