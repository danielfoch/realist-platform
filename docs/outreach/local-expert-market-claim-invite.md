## Email

Hi {{first_name}},

We are opening a small founding group of local experts and meetup hosts for The Canadian Real Estate Investor Podcast / Realist.ca.

The idea is simple: when investors use Realist to underwrite properties in your market, we want a trusted local person attached to that demand.

That means:

- investor referrals in your claimed market
- full underwriting context attached to the lead
- visibility as a local expert / meetup host
- a CRM seat to manage follow-up and deal status
- no monthly fee
- referral fee only on closed referred transactions

The first version is intentionally narrow. We are not building a paid directory or a generic networking group. We are matching real investor demand from Realist.ca to credible local operators who can actually help.

The workflow is:

1. You claim a market.
2. You sign the referral agreement before claiming leads.
3. Investor leads in that market get routed with the deal analysis attached.
4. You work the lead in the Realist partner CRM.
5. You keep the status updated so we can keep routing clean leads to the right people.

The CRM backend is GHL-backed today, and the plan is to connect this into Homie over time so follow-up, reminders, and outcome reporting become less manual.

If you want to claim your market, start here:

https://realist.ca/about/local-experts

The realtor partner signup flow is here:

https://realist.ca/partner/onboarding?type=realtor

This is invite-only while we seed the first markets. If you want in, claim your market and reply with the city/region you want to own.
